Deep Fried Hot Dog

Description

The world's best hot dog. It's almost like a regular hot dog, except it's dunked in butter, rolled with bacon, and deep fried to a crisp

Ingredients

  • One Hot Dog
  • Five Strips of Bacon
  • Three Cups of Butter

Directions

  1. Bring butter to a boil
  2. Warm Oil
  3. Place Hot Dog into oil - fry until golden brown
  4. Wrap Hot Dog with Bacon, skewer ends
  5. Fry Hot Dog again until golden brown
  6. Enjoy!
